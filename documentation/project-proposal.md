## Performing Arts Manager - Mobile
---
A mobile frontend for [Performing Arts Manager](https://github.com/Mindstormman06/performing-arts-manager) built in React Native.

The intention for this app is to provide cast and crew with a easy way to check everything they need to know about their responsibilities in a show while on the go

### Features
- View rehearsals you're scheduled for
- Check all of your assigned costumes, props, and other items
- Review your blocking for each scene
